Visit Chapel Hill

A small college town offering much more than meets the eye.

Top three activities to do at Chapel Hill

Historical sightseeing

You can check out historical and culturally significant locations! These include the Bell Tower and the Old Well, among other interesting landmarks.

Visit the Museum

You can visit the Ackland Art museum or Smithsonian Planetarium! Both are an excellent way to have a fun and educational time!

See a sports game

You can catch a sports game! Chapel Hill is known for its athletics programs as well as its academics, so you can catch top notch college sports here at UNC.
